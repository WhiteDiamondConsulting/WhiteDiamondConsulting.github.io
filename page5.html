Welcome to White Diamond Consulting, your premier partner for dental practice success.

 We are a team of two seasoned dental industry experts who are passionate about helping practices thrive. With over 60 years of combined experience in various roles within the dental industry, including Hygiene Coordinator, Dental Assistant, Treatment Coordinator, Accounts Manager, Office Manager, Clinical Director, Regional Manager, HR Manager and General Manager, we understand the challenges and opportunities facing dental practices today.

Our journey began in 1995 when we met as Dental Assistants, and over the years, we have worked together in different offices, eventually managing a 16-op clinic. In 2008, we became instructors and clinical directors over multiple clinics and their systems, and through another purchase in 2015, we both went our own ways and eventually General Managers for two different corporations. Our successful partnership in management for over 10 years has enabled us to develop a unique perspective and expertise in simplifying IPAC, health and safety, compliance, team building, and practice management.

At White Diamond Consulting, we specialize in providing customized solutions to empower dental practices to provide the best possible care to their patients. We believe that the success of a dental practice depends on a strong and cohesive team, and we work with our clients to develop strategies that improve communication, collaboration, and accountability.

If you're looking for a dental consulting company with a wealth of experience and a passion for excellence, look no further than White Diamond Consulting. Let us help you find clarity to take your dental practice to the next level.
