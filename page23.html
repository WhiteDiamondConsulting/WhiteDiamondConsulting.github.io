New Possibilities in Support and Training

 Support Training for New Dental Owners, Managers, and Staff: We will provide comprehensive training and support to help new dental practice owners, managers, and staff members succeed. Our training programs will cover a range of topics, including leadership and management, financial management, marketing, branding, patient care, communication, staff training and development.

 Hygiene Coordinator and Treatment Coordinator Support: We will provide specialized support to hygiene coordinators and treatment coordinators to help them optimize their roles and increase production for the practice. Our services will include training on patient communication, scheduling, case presentation,  treatment acceptance, as well as ongoing coaching and mentoring.

By offering these services and solutions, White Diamond Consulting aims to help dental practices optimize their performance, achieve their goals, and thrive in today's competitive healthcare environment.
